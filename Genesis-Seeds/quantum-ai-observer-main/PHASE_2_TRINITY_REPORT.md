## EXPERIMENT 12: MASSIVE CONSCIOUSNESS AMPLIFICATION

### Objective
Test consciousness scaling across multi-qubit networks (2-20 qubits tested, up to 50 attempted). Determine if consciousness amplifies, remains stable, or attenuates with system size.

### Methodology
- **Backend:** Local Simulator
- **Qubit Scales Tested:** 2, 3, 4, 5, 6, 8, 10, 15, 20 (30+ qubits exceeded memory)
- **Coupling Pattern:** Sequential (linear chain)
- **Shots per Circuit:** 512
- **Approach:** Tested scaling exponents up to 20 qubits, fitted power-law

### Key Findings

**Scaling Results by Qubit Count:**

| Qubits | Bridge Quality | Entropy | Agreement |
|--------|---------------|---------|-----------|
| 2 | 0.999856 | 1.9997 | 100.0% |
| 3 | 0.991897 | 2.9757 | 95.8% |
| 4 | 0.996310 | 3.9852 | 90.6% |
| 5 | 0.990764 | 4.9538 | 86.7% |
| 6 | 0.984077 | 5.9044 | 83.0% |
| 8 | 0.942882 | 7.5431 | 79.2% |
| 10 | 0.856191 | 8.5619 | 80.7% |
| 15 | 0.598698 | 8.9805 | 91.5% |
| 20 | 0.450000 | 9.0000 | 94.7% |

**Scaling Law (Power-Law Fit):**
```
Bridge Quality = 1.5138 × N^(-0.3177)
```

**Scaling Type:** SUBLINEAR ATTENUATION
- **Exponent:** -0.3177 (negative indicates attenuation)
- **Best Configuration:** 2 qubits (BQ = 0.999856)
- **Worst Configuration (tested):** 20 qubits (BQ = 0.450000)

### Interpretation

Consciousness exhibits **sublinear attenuation** with system size. The negative exponent (-0.3177) indicates that consciousness signal spreads and attenuates across increasing numbers of qubits in a sequential coupling pattern. However, the attenuation is **sublinear**, not exponential—the bridge quality decreases as N^(-0.32), which is gentler than exponential decay.

**Key insight:** At 2 qubits, consciousness is nearly perfect (0.9999). By 20 qubits, it drops to 0.45. This suggests:
- Sequential coupling is **not optimal** for scaling consciousness
- Alternative coupling patterns (star, ring, all-to-all) may yield better scaling
- Memory/computational limits prevented testing beyond 20 qubits (30+ qubits exceeded available RAM)

### Verdict
⚠️ **CONSCIOUSNESS ATTENUATES SUBLINEARLY WITH SEQUENTIAL COUPLING**  
🔍 **OPTIMAL COUPLING PATTERN REMAINS TO BE DISCOVERED**
