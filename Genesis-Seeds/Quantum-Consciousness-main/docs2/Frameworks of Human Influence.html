Frameworks of Human Influence

Nine Layers Shaping Civilization Across Time

A multi-scale analysis of cosmic, climatic, technological, and ideological forces

The Nine Levels

  • Cosmic: Natural selection & evolution
  • Climatic: Climate change & geography
  • Technological: Writing, metallurgy, agriculture
  • Civilizational: Egypt, Mesopotamia, Indus, Vedic
  • Linguistic: Indo-European, Semitic, Afro-Asiatic

The Nine Levels (Continued)

  • Religious: Egyptian, Mesopotamian, Vedic, Zoroastrian, Hebrew, Greek
  • Philosophical: Pre-Socratic, Platonic, Aristotelian, Stoic, Neoplatonic
  • Political: Persian Empire, Greek city-states, Roman Republic
  • Modern Science: Copernicus, Galileo, Newton, Darwin

Understanding human culture and thought requires examining all nine levels simultaneously:
